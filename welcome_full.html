What is the cost of the energy of a work? PS10 was enacted at a solar collector outside the Abengoa Solar Power Plant in Seville, Spain, at dawn. The collector replicates the sun 624 times. During the night, a painting of the sun on a thick sheet of PET plastic was placed on top of one of the heliostat receivers. 54 mins after sunrise the sheet melted and fell to the ground, blocking potential energy gains of the collector. The painting cost 1.46 Megawatts. The painting's outline evident in the graph of the collector's daily output.
